Finally solved this:

First step was to get lk to say anything about devicetrees at boot.
Turns out â€œLK_DEBUGâ€ must be set to â€œ1â€ in the recipe, or all â€œdprintf(INFOâ€ messages are optimized away.

Once this was in place, the devicetree selection process showed itâ€™s face and it was fairly obvious to follow it to â€œboard_override_hardware_subtype()â€ and in turn â€œWP_BOARD_PROBEâ€

So, it turns out the mangOH boards are dynamically probed for, and it can be enabled with the following bbappend file:

lk_1.3.0.bbappend:

LK_DEBUG := "1"
WP_BOARD_PROBE := "1"

# Debug
do_compile_prepend() {
    echo "OEMAKE: ${EXTRA_OEMAKE}"
}
