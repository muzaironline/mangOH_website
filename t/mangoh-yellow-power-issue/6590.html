MangOH Yellow Power Issue

mangOH Yellow
curtisleo March 24, 2021, 4:58am 1

Issue : Power cycling Yellow board will cause the system to “lock-up” and requires pressing the reset button to allow the system to restart.

Reproduction steps : Plug USB or Expansion power in(Pin 1 & 15), switch on, powers up fine. Disconnect power and reinsert USB or Expansion power in any time from 1s to 15s, reapply power and the board will not boot. Cycling the power switch does nothing. Pressing the reset button will restart the board and comes up.

Looking at the schematics, it appears the power does go through the BQ25601 and is causing this issue. I do not have a battery connected.

Functionality : Apply 5V on, disconnect 5V, turns off.
