Welcome!

This forum is used by the community around Project mangOH to help one another to use mangOH, team up and contribute to mangOH.

Use the category mangOH Green for any discussion or question related to the usage of mangOH Green.
Use the category mangOH Red for any discussion or question related to the usage of mangOH Red.
Use the category Build your own for any discussion or question related to building or designing your own hardware based on mangOH designs.

Is it your first time?

If this is your first time on this forum, don’t forget to say hello and introduce yourself briefly in the water cooler discussions category so that other members can better know you.

Happy mangOH experience!
