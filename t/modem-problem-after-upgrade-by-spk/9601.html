I used one click exe to update fw in windows platform.

The userapp partition will not erased by swiflash tool in the new FW.
You need to downgrade to some old fw and use swiflash tool to erase userapp partition .
E.g. wp76 → downgrade to r12 or before

Wp77 → downgrade to r11 or before
