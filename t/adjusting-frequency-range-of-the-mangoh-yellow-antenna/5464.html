Adjusting frequency range of the mangOH Yellow antenna

mangOH Yellow mangOH Yellow antenna
Carles October 22, 2020, 11:26am 1

The frequency range of the mangOH Yellow chip antenna can be changed an adapted to your needs quite easily by just tuning the matching network. Without changing the antenna part or having to make a custom design you will get the antenna to work on the frequencies you need. This is enable by a new generation of chip antenna components called ‘antenna boosters’. You can use Cadence AWR, Optenni or similar tools to automatically tune the matching network.
