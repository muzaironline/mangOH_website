awneil August 9, 2018, 12:49pm 2
Alice:

As I know the MangOH boards has not any sensor install by default

No, that’s not true

  • mangOH Red has Accelerometer/Gyroscope, Pressure and Light sensors:
    mangOH® Red: the smallest mangOH platform supports 10 years battery lifetime

  • mangOH Green has Built-in IMU (accelerometer and gyroscope): mangOH® Green: the most flexible and expandable mangOH platform

Alice:

Do you know any sensor provider which we can use with your boards?

The boards also have standard interfaces - I2C, SPI, UART, etc - which you can use to connect sensors.

Do be sure to pay attention to voltage levels!

Have you looked at the range of available IoT Cards: IoT Expansion Cards: adding sensors and networks connectivity

EDIT

And don’t forget the Arduino-compatible connectors on Green, and Raspberry-Pi connector on Red …
